Algorithm Development

Today’s algorithms are usually complex pieces of code that can take large chunks of data and process them into usable pieces of information. These algorithms can be AI-based, ML-based, or something entirely different, but they all process data in one form or another. The advantage of these algorithms is that they can significantly improve your business’s efficiency if they are employed properly.

Developing and deploying these algorithms is no easy task and it can be very expensive to hire someone to do it internally. That’s where I can help out. If you want a custom algorithm built but don’t want to stand up a new team to do it, give me a call. Whether it’s something as simple as a clustering system to quickly categorize data, a moderately complicated object detection algorithm, or even a complex multi-layered self-supervised system that mitigates the effects of a data-starved algorithm, I can help you find a solution that boosts your processes efficiency at a reasonable price.

Data Engineering

I have spent years working on extract, transform, and load (ETL) pipelines so I understand how frustrating these tasks can be. Computers are notoriously finicky with data formats and types, so ensuring that all the pieces of the puzzle fit together is a difficult task. The quality of all types of complex algorithms depend on the quality of their training data, so it’s imperative to ensure that their data pipeline is properly constructed. I can help construct any piece or whole of a pipeline and ensure that it will fit into your downstream systems, especially if I am also constructing an algorithm for you.

But not all organizations are going to need complex algorithms with specific data requirements. Some organizations might have a ton of repetitive work on a computer but not have any data engineers on their team. I can also help in these instances by automating processes for an organization. These can be anything from a system to reformat thousands of photos automatically to automatically fetching data from APIs and processing them into usable reports.

Specialties

  • Construction of Custom ETL Pipelines
  • Handling Multiple Disparate Data Sources
  • Customized Augmentations
